## Ingredients
- 1360g Chicken Thigh
- 5 tbsp soy sauce
- 2 tbsp dark soy sauce
- 4 tbsp honey
- 4 tbsp mirin
- 2 tbsp ginger paste
- 1 tbsp garlic paste
- Cornstarch slurry - 2 tbsp cornstarch & 4 tbsp cold water

**Yum Yum Sauce**
- ½ cup mayo
- ½ cup greek yoghurt
- 5 tbsp sriracha
- salt, pepper, garlic to taste

## Method

1. Dump all and mix in slow cooker
2. High 4 hours
3. Dump 2.5 cups of cooked rice into the slow cooker and mix with chicken
4. Chopped spring onion and sesame seeds to garnish
