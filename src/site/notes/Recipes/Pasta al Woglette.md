## Ingredients
- 2 tsp salt
- 1 tsp black pepper
- 2 onion
- 4 good sized bay leaves
- 1/4 cup olive oil
- 2 tbsp mince garlic
- 1/2 tsp red pepper flakes
- 3 tbs Italian herbs
- 500ml chicken stock
- 2x 810g cans crushed tomato or Passatta
- 1 Jar tomato paste
- 1 kg beef mince (or half pork)
- 1 packet Italian Sausage (with fennel)

## Method
1. Olive oil get hot in stock pot
2. Chuck in red chili flakes and 30 seconds later onion
3. Saute onion for a bit and chuck in garlic
4. When onion soggy, brown mince
5. When mince brown, chuck the rest of the stuff in (not sosig though)
6. Get to simmer and turn it right down low
7. Every 30 min or so stir
8. 3/4 hours of stirring in, chuck in sosig - can brown in pan on high first if you can be assed
9. 4-6 hours total should be good
