## Ingredients

- 5 cups of flour
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp yeast
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 2 cup of water

## Method

1. Mix dry ingredients
2. Add water 1/2 cup at time
3. knead for 10 min
4. let overnight to rise

Sauce

2 tbsp olive oil

2 tbps garlic

800g crush tomato

2 tsp salt

1 large Onion

1 tbsp italian seasoning

Medium high stove put in oil till hot

Put in chili flakes, onion, garlic- soften the onion

Chuck in mince, brown mince

Put in crush tomato, stock, paste, herb mix, salt, pepper, bay leaves

Bring to simmer and put on the lowest low

Maintain simmer and stir every 30 min for 4 hours
