## Ingredients
- ~14 tbsp Olive Oil (6tbsp for chicken 8 tbsp for celery)
- 2 Medium-Large Onions diced
- Salt
- 1kg Chicken Thighs cute into 1 inch pieces
- 2 tsp ground Turmeric
- Salt & Black Pepper
- 2 heads of Celery sliced into half inch wide pieces
- 2 bunches of Parsley, stems removed, finely chopped
- 1 bunch mint, stems removed, finely chopped
- 6 tbs lemon juice
- ½ tsp saffron steeped in ~1 cup of hot water

## Method
Chicken / Main pot
1. Heat large pot medium-high with olive oil
2. Add onion and cook, stir occasionally, until golden brown
3. Sprinkle Salt & turn down to medium
4. Add 2tsp Salt, ½ Tsp Pepper & the turmeric and chicken
5. Cook 3-5 min until chicken takes on colour, then turn to low

Celery
1. Heat wide pan medium-high with olive oil
2. Add celery & ½ tsp Salt, cook for 10 min stir occasionally until slightly fried and reduced in size a bit
3. Turn to medium and add parsley, cook for 5 min and stire often
4. Turn to low and add mint, cook ~1 min stir the whole time
5. Take off heat and add to chicken

Main Pot
1. Turn to medium, add saffron, water & lemon juice, & 1 tsp salt
2. Once a slight bubble occurs, turn to low and simmer for ~30 min or until it all comes together and the celery is soft but not mushy
